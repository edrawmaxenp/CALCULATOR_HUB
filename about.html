About ElectroCalc Hub

ElectroCalc Hub is built to help students and engineers perform practical component-level calculations quickly, with clear assumptions and useful outputs.

Mission

Deliver a focused engineering toolkit for daily circuit design work, lab validation, and design review preparation. The goal is speed with technical clarity.

Who This Is For

  • Diploma and undergraduate electronics students
  • Power electronics and embedded hardware engineers
  • Design teams preparing BOM and thermal checks
  • Anyone validating calculations before prototyping

What You Can Do

  • Run core analog and passive component calculations
  • Design Buck, Boost, and Buck-Boost converter estimates
  • Review losses, thermal paths, and heatsink suggestions
  • Generate quick PDF reports for documentation

Study Focus

  • Circuit fundamentals and component behavior
  • Power-stage operation and switching stress
  • Thermal management and derating methods
  • Practical checklist-based engineering workflow

Design Principles

  • Simple inputs with useful engineering outputs
  • Safety margin guidance wherever possible
  • Readable formulas and practical assumptions
  • Fast access from desktop and mobile

Built for electronics students and engineers.
